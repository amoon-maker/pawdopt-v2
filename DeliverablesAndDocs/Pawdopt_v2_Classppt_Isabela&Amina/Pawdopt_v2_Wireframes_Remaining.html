Why is this required?
Pawdopt v2 is built for shelter clients who need every listing to be trustworthy. Before your pet appears in search results, a platform administrator reviews your document to confirm your pet is healthy and properly cared for. This protects both adopters and animals from scams and unsafe placements.
Accepted documents
✓ Vaccination record (from vet)
✓ Health certificate
✓ Vet visit summary (within 12 months)
✓ Shelter intake documentation
